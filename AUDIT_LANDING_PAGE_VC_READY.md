## APPENDICE A - REFERENCE NORMATIVO

### EU AI Act (Regulation 2024/1689)
- **Art. 50-52:** Transparency obligations for limited-risk AI
- **Art. 14:** Human oversight requirements
- **Art. 9:** Risk management system

### GDPR (Regulation 2016/679)
- **Art. 13-14:** Information to data subjects
- **Art. 25:** Data protection by design and by default
- **Art. 32:** Security of processing

### EU Food Safety Regulations
- **Reg. 178/2002:** General food law
- **Reg. 852/2004:** Food hygiene
- **Reg. 1169/2011:** Food information to consumers (allergens)

## APPENDICE B - GLOSSARY

**AI Provider:** Entity that develops and places AI system on market (ChefCode holding)  
**AI Deployer:** Entity that uses AI system (restaurants/customers)  
**Limited-Risk AI:** AI systems with transparency obligations only  
**High-Risk AI:** AI systems requiring conformity assessment  
**Human-in-the-loop:** Human oversight and final decision-making authority  
**Decision-support system:** AI that suggests, human decides
